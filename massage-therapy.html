Massage Therapy FAQ

What types of massage do you offer?+

Our therapists tailor each session — relaxation, tension-relief, and recovery-focused work — based on how your body feels that day.

How long are sessions?+

We offer multiple session lengths; most guests love 60 or 90 minutes. Check the booking page for current options.

Should I arrive early?+

Arriving 10 minutes early lets you settle in, breathe, and start your session fully relaxed.

Yes — many guests pair massage with a facial or body sculpting for a full reset. Ask us about building your visit.
